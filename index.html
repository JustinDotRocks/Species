About Manuels River

Manuels River is a community-driven initiative focused on conserving the natural and cultural heritage of the area. This app aims to enhance the visitor experience, providing interactive and informative insights into the diverse species found along the river and its surroundings.

Manuels River Community Inc. is dedicated to preserving the river's ecological integrity and promoting environmental education through various programs and activities.

About the Project

Species; a Species Catalogue, is a dynamic web application designed to enhance the exploration and learning experience at Manuels River.

It offers an interactive platform where users can discover and learn about various species found along the river through detailed descriptions, images, and maps. Built with a focus on user engagement and education, the Species Catalogue serves as a bridge between nature enthusiasts and the rich biodiversity of Manuels River, promoting conservation and awareness through digital innovation.

Whether you're planning a visit or simply curious about the natural world, our catalogue provides an accessible and informative resource for all ages.
